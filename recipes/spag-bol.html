Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

Description

The classic italian dish is so easy to make.

It can be kept simple or embelished with vegetables of your choice.

Ingredients

(serves 4)

  • Spaghetti (fresh or dried) 500g
  • Beef mince 500g
  • Tomatoes chopped (fresh or tinned) 500g
  • onion large x1
  • mushrooms 250g
  • garlic bulbs x3
  • cheddar cheese, grated 150g
  • parmesan cheese, grated 100g
  • oregano 1 teaspoon
  • rosemary 1 sprig

Steps

  1. Fry the onion, garlic and mince.
  2. Add the tomatoes and herbs. Simmer for 15 mins to make the bolognese sauce.
  3. Meanwhile, boil the pasta in salted water for 10-12 minutes.
  4. When the pasta is cooked, drain and mix into the bolognese sauce.
  5. Sprinkle the the grated cheeses and serve.
