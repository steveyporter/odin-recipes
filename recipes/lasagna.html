Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is a wonderfully simple dish that can be prepared within an hour.

It can be frozen in individual batches and cooked from frozen.

Ingredients

(serves 4)

  • Pasta sheets x4
  • Beef mince 500g
  • onion large x1
  • garlic bulbs x3
  • cheddar cheese, grated 150g
  • parmesan cheese, grated 100g
  • flour 100g
  • milk 200ml
  • flour 60g
  • oregano 1 teaspoon
  • rosemary 1 sprig

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C.
  2. Make a cheese sauce from the milk and flour and most of the cheese; keep some cheese aside.
  3. Fry the onion, garlic and mince.
  4. Transfer a quarter of the mince to an oven-proof dish.
  5. Add salt, pepper and a quarter of the herbs.
  6. Add a pasta sheet and top with a quarter of the cheese sauce.
  7. Repeat the previous 3 steps until all the ingredients are used up.
  8. Sprinkle the top of the lasagne with the remainder of the grated cheeses.
  9. Cook in the oven for 45 minutes.
