Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Only a few ingredients needed for this super-tasty italian dish.

It can be made in minutes.

Ingredients

(serves 4)

  • Spaghetti (fresh or dried) 500g
  • Pancetta or diced bacon 500g
  • Eggs x3
  • garlic bulbs x3
  • cheddar cheese, grated 150g
  • parmesan cheese, grated 100g
  • Salt 1 teaspoon
  • Black pepper 1 teaspoon

Steps

  1. Fry the garlic and pancetta.
  2. Break the eggs into a mixing bowl and beat lightly. Add three quarters of the cheese and all of the salt and pepper.
  3. Meanwhile, boil the pasta in salted water for 10-12 minutes.
  4. When the pasta is cooked, drain and mix into the pancetta in the pan, keeping the pasta water.
  5. turn off the heat and add the egg mixture, tossing the ingredients gently
  6. If the mixture looks too dry, add some pasta water.
  7. Sprinkle the the remaining grated cheeses and serve.
